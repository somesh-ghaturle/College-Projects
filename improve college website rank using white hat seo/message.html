Dr. Prasanna Zade
(Principal)

My Dear Students,

It is my privilege and pleasure to welcome you all to Datta Meghe Institute of Engineering, Technology & Research [DMIETR}. Established in 2008, the Institution was started with a vision by our Chairman, Hon’ble Shri. Dattaji Meghe, Member of Parliament, Lok Sabha & within a short period of time, it has already set high standards in Technical Education under the able guidance and leadership of Hon. Shri. Sagarji Meghe, Treasurer, NYSS. At DMIETR, we believe in giving complete education by concentrating on all the aspects of all-round professional development of our students. Our goal as educators is to support all students academically and socially. We make every effort to make all students successful and literate members of society and be able to succeed in the increasingly advancing world in which they will live and work.

High academic excellence coupled with moral rectitude, intellectual integrity, sound aesthetic sense, etc, is the essence of our institution. With the help of latest technology & methods in teaching, we enable the students to utilize their potential and channelize their efforts to make the best of education. We inculcate value based education and sculpt our students as good competitors in the corporate world. The Faculty of the Institution plays a vital role in moulding the young minds of the students and in upholding the academic tradition of the Institution.

The pleasing and peaceful environment, in which the college is located, helps the students in focusing more on their studies. Well equipped laboratories and workshop with most modern and sophisticated instruments/machineries are provided to impart high quality education to the students. Well furnished hostel facilities are provided for both the boys and girls. Invited talks by external experts in various fields of Engineering and Management are organized on a regular basis for the students to enlarge their horizon of knowledge. We believe in improving the quality of students by implementing various activities like language development, insisting on student discipline and encouraging the students by encouraging them to participate in extra curricular activities like sports apart from their academics. To augment the student performance, various extra-curricular, co-curricular activities are conducted regularly. To nourish the knowledge of faculty members and students, each department organizes National Conferences, Workshops and Student’s Technical Symposium.

We at DMIETR believe that honesty, hard work, and discipline together form the ladder for success and try to inculcate these habits in our students. I wish you all the success in your endeavours & a pleasant stay at the Institution.
